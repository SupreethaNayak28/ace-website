About Us

ASSOCIATION OF COMPUTER ENGINEERS

The Association of Computer Engineers (ACE) at NMAMIT Nitte is a student association dedicated to fostering co-curricular engagement and connections among Computer Science and Engineering students. Our mission is to unearth hidden talents and empower every member to thrive beyond the classroom. Since our inception, we've been actively involved in:
1. Hosting technical events and workshops on diverse topics beyond the curriculum.
2. Celebration of Freshers' Day, Farewell Day and festivals.
3. Organizing cultural events and competitions.
4. Promoting web development and computer literacy among school students.
5. Hosting technical talks and facilitating alumni interactions.
6. Encouraging active participation in co-curricular activities and sports.
